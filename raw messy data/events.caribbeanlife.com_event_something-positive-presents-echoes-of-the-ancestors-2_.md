# Something Positive Presents: Echoes of the Ancestors

June 6, 7 pm – 9 pm. $30

![120937769_10157559841266188_4146309358703966941_n_1c58ae](https://events.amny.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/120937769_10157559841266188_4146309358703966941_n_1c58ae.jpg?quality=51&w=960)

- [Event website](https://www.eventbrite.com/e/something-positive-presents-echoes-of-the-ancestors-tickets-1988357170823?aff=oddtdtcreator)
- [347–600–2189](tel:3476002189)

**Echoes of the Ancestors** is a vibrant evening of dance, music, and storytelling inspired by the traditions of the African Diaspora.

This program features both new and returning works that honor ancestral legacies while exploring how those histories continue to move through the body today. Through dynamic choreography and richly layered performance, **Something Positive**, a New York City-based performing arts company, brings past and present into conversation on one stage.
