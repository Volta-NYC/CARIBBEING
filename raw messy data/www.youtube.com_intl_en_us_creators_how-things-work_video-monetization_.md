# How to make money on YouTube

When it comes to turning your ideas into income, you’ve got
options.

From traditional ads to selling merchandise to memberships for
your fans, there are a lot of ways to use your channel and videos
to make money.


## YouTube Partner Program

There are so many formats available to make content on YouTube,
such as Shorts, live streaming and long-form video, and we want
to make sure Creators can get rewarded for making all of them.
The YouTube Partner Program lets qualifying Creators tap into
different ways to earn revenue, as well as gives access to
resources like 1:1 support and the Copyright Match Tool.
Creators are building successful businesses of their own with
employees and full-fledged operations and are a significant
share of the more than $50B we’ve paid out to creators, artists
and media companies over the last three years.

YouTube Partner Program: How to Make Money on YouTube


Are you eligible?


To qualify for the YouTube Partner Program, you need
to be in good standing with YouTube, follow all the
[YouTube monetization policies](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/1311392), and live in a country/region where
[the YouTube Partner Program is available](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/7101720).


Creators in
[these countries/regions](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/13429240#expandedyppcountries)
are now able to qualify for the YouTube Partner
Program sooner:


- Apply to the program and unlock benefits like fan
funding and select YouTube Shopping features at
the following eligibility criteria:
  - 500 subscribers with 3 public uploads in the
     last 90 days, and either 3K valid public watch
     hours in the last 12 months OR 3M public
     Shorts views in the last 90 days.
- Unlock even more benefits including revenue
sharing from ads and YouTube Premium when you hit:
  - 1,000 subscribers and either 4K valid public
     watch hours in the last 12 months OR 10M valid
     public Shorts views in the last 90 days.

All creators will go through a standard review
process upon applying to ensure their channel meets
our policies and guidelines. Some features may have
additional eligibility criteria.


[Learn more about the YouTube Partner Program](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/72851)

The benefits of being in the YouTube Partner Program


The YouTube Partner Program is a comprehensive
partner program available to Creators, delivering
support, monetization opportunities and more. Once
you’re in the program, you can start earning money
from ads on long-form videos, ads between short-form
videos and from YouTube Premium subscribers watching
your content. You can also use Shopping to offer
products to your audience and access Fan Funding
revenue streams including Super Chat, Super Thanks,
Super Stickers and channel memberships. What’s more,
creators in the program are guaranteed access to our
Creator Support team with one-to-one help over email
and chat and automatic copyright flags of reuploaded
content through the Copyright Match Tool. We're
always exploring ways to make the YouTube Partner
Program the most rewarding and supportive experience
for our Creators.
