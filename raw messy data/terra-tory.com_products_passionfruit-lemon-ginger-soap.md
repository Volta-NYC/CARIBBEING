Crafted for MaryFest, this limited release embodies cannabis as ritual, not escape. Hempseed oil leads, rich and green, grounding the skin. Passionfruit replaces coconut oil, creating a silkier, gentler lather that respects dry, sensitive skin. Geranium and ginger rise softly, bright yet warm, layered with Cedarwood and a quiet trace of lemon. A bright, juicy cleanse that softens as it refreshes.

The natural fruit sugars help create a creamy, moisturizing lather that doesn’t strip the skin, while a vibrant blend of lemon and ginger essential oils brings a subtle, uplifting warmth to the experience.

Beneath the scent, your skin is met with what it actually needs: a nourishing base of raw shea butter, hempseed, avocado, and rice bran oils. The result is skin that feels clean, soft, and comfortably hydrated — never tight, never dry.

This is your morning bar. The one that wakes you up gently, without taking anything from your skin.
