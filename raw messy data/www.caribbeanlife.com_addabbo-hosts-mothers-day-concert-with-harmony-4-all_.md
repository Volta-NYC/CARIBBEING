# Addabbo hosts Mother’s Day concert with Harmony 4 All

By Nelson A. KingPosted on [May 1, 2026](https://www.caribbeanlife.com/addabbo-hosts-mothers-day-concert-with-harmony-4-all/)

In a celebration of motherhood and community, State Sen. Joseph P. Addabbo, Jr., invites residents to a free Mother’s Day jazz concert honoring the spirit of moms everywhere.

Addabbo said on Monday, April 27, that the show will showcase musical group Harmony 4 All and that the performance is open to all ages.

It will be held on May 1, 2026, from 7 p.m. – 8 p.m. at Sacred Heart Church, 83-17 78th Ave., Glendale, Queens, NY 11385.

He said Harmony 4 All, founded by brother-sister duo Joshua and Bianca Quddus, provides musical education to underserved students in local schools, “recognizing its vital role in emotional and intellectual development.

“They remove financial hurdles and provide access to musical materials that help students develop their love for music and creativity,” he said.

Co-founder Bianca Quddus said, “We’re so excited to invite you to a very special evening as we celebrate music, community, and the spirit of Mother’s Day together.

“Join us for our Mother’s Day Concert — a heartfelt musical experience featuring talented young musicians and a warm, welcoming atmosphere for all,” he said. “This is more than a concert — it’s a moment to come together, reflect, and enjoy the power of music across generations.”

Addabbo said the Mother’s Day Concert will include standard jazz favorites that “uplift and celebrate the spirit and strength of mothers, grandmothers, stepmothers, godmothers, and motherly women who lift generations and lead families.

“The church is intended to be an open place, communicating the sentiment that music and community can unite and improve families in celebration,” he said. “Music has a way of honoring the people who shape our lives, particularly the moms who love us and support us with resilience and grace.

“It’s an amazing chance to take time and thank the amazing moms from all over our community. They’re the foundation of our society,” the State Senator added. “This concert is one way we can honor the sacrifices moms make and the joy they extend daily. Thanks to Harmony 4 All and Sacred Heart Church for their support of the community.”
