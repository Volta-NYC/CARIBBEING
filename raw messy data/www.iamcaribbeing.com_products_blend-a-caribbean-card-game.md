**Blend – The Ultimate Caribbean-Inspired Card Game**

Turn up the energy at your next game night with _Blend_—the fast-paced card game that brings Caribbean culture to the table. Designed for players who love to laugh, compete, and celebrate the islands, _Blend_ is your new go-to for unforgettable moments.

The game features three card types—Guess That Word, Trivia, and True or False—each designed to challenge your knowledge, creativity, and quick thinking. Every turquoise card is worth one point, and trust us, it gets competitive fast.

Whether you're at a family lime, a backyard cookout, or just vibing with friends, _Blend_ delivers nonstop fun and cultural pride in every round.

**Highlights:**

- Inspired by Caribbean culture, created for everyone

- Great for parties, family nights, or travel

- Ages 12 and up \| 4+ players recommended

- Quick to learn, impossible to forget
