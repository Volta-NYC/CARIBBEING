You will be notified by email with an order confirmation by us.

Can I change my shipping address after my order is placed?


Yes. Contact us immediately at [**hello@terra-tory.com**](mailto:hello@terra-tory.com "mailto:hello@terra-tory.com") with your order number in the email subject.

## Shipping & Returns

Do you ship internationally?


We only ship in the U.S. at this time.

When will my order be shipped?


Orders placed **Monday** through **Thursday** get shipped within 1-2 days.

Orders placed on Friday get shipped the following week.

_See our_ [_**full shipping policy here**_](https://terra-tory.com/pages/shipping-policy "Shipping Policy") _._

How long will it take until I receive my order?


We currently use UPS Ground, which is estimated to getting your order delivered within 1-5 days, depending on your location.

What is your return policy?


All products must be returned in the condition is was received within 10 days of purchase.

_See our full shipping/return policy_ [_**here**_](https://terra-tory.com/pages/shipping-policy "Shipping Policy").
