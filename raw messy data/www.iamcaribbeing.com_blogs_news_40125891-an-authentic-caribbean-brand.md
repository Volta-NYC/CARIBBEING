# An Authentic Caribbean Brand.

![An Authentic Caribbean Brand.](https://www.iamcaribbeing.com/cdn/shop/articles/CaribBeing_House_1445x.jpg?v=1499893055)

What started as a private collection two summers ago during our FlatPoP! series has now become a beloved heritage brand. CaribBeing launched its first collection at BROOKLYN DESIGNS in 2015. Our signature tanks, t-shirts and totes are proudly designed + made with <3 in the USA.
