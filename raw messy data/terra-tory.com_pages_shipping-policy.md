## TERRA-TORY Shipping Policy

1. Our ship days: Monday - Thursday only.
2. Orders placed on Friday get shipped the following Monday.
3. We do not ship on holidays.
4. Please allow 3-4 business days for your order to be processed and shipped.
5. Shipping and Billing information must be identical.
6. During this difficult time, please be sure that the shipping address provided is available to receive your package(s) to avoid redelivery fees. Please double check your delivery address. You must provide an address you know you will be receiving delivery. This includes missing apartment number.
7. You will receive order confirmation and/or tracking number/information once your order has been processed.
8. If you submitted an **incorrect** or **insufficient address**, your order will be shipped back to us. We will then contact you via email. _Additional shipping invoice_ will be sent before reshipping your order. Once invoice is paid, we will proceed with reshipment.
9. Failure to pay invoice within 5 business days will result in cancelation and full refund of order.
