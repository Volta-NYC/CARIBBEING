**CRANBERRY CLASSIC**

The Cranberry Classic is a crunchy, nutty, granola delight! Packed full of walnuts, pecans, almonds and cashews and smattering of 🍁Fall favorite fruit - dried cranberries. You won't be disappointed with this aromatic, buttery🧈 goodness! birthday, holiday...pretty much any time of year! Add to your favorite yogurt and you've got breakfast with a bang💥!

**CHAI TIME**

Breathe in the heady mix of classic chai spices - cinnamon, cardamom, ginger and nutmeg, and let Chai Time take you to India. There's no fruit in this crunchy, slightly spicy granola mix. Just generous heapings of almonds, coconut🥥, cashews and pistachios mixed with our heady spice blend and old-fashioned oats.

**FALL BACK**

Without a doubt, Fall is one of the best parts about living in the Northeast! The Fall Back Mix is inspired by the gorgeous colors and flavors of Fall - think Pumpkin Spice latte☕ with a granola twist! Hearty oats loving paired with hazelnuts, pumpkin seeds, walnuts and pecans, bathed in butter🧈 and maple syrup.

**!SAY OLÉ!**

Celebrate Mexico with this gluten-free granola mix that's packed with hearty whole-rolled oats, coconut🥥, cashews, peanuts🥜, pecans and dried bananas🍌. A healthy dose of classic Mexican spices adds a little kick!
