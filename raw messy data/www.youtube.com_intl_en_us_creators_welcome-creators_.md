Staying Safe on YouTube: Policies and Tools for Creators


Guidelines for good


Our policies and guidelines were created to support
everyone on YouTube: Creators and viewers of all
ages, races, cultures, and more. We ask everyone to
follow these guidelines so that we can all continue
to enjoy YouTube together.


[Read our Policies & Guidelines](https://www.youtube.com/creators/how-things-work/policies-guidelines)

A place for all Creators


We’ve taken a close look at our policies to make
sure YouTube protects everyone in our community.
We’ve made changes like improving the way you can
filter comments to help cut down on offensive
content, and we’ve given Creators a place to share
info about their race, gender, ethnicity, and sexual
orientation.


[Read Inclusivity Update](https://blog.youtube/news-and-events/make-youtube-more-inclusive-platform/)

No room for hate or harassment


We work hard to protect everyone in the YouTube
community. We strongly believe that there is no
place for hate or harassment here and have Hate
Speech and Harassment & Cyberbullying policies
in place to protect viewers and Creators.


We partner with experts and many different
communities to write our policies to ensure everyone
is included and everyone’s voice is heard.


[See Hate Speech policy](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/2801939)

[See Harassment policy](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/2802268)
