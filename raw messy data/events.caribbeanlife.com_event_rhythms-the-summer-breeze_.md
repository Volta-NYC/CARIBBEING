# Rhythms & The Summer Breeze

August 22, 5:30 pm. $45 - $75

![Instagram-BlancheButterflyMedia-RhythmsAndTheSummerBreeze-Flyer](https://events.amny.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/Instagram-BlancheButterflyMedia-RhythmsAndTheSummerBreeze-Flyer-e1778474181396.jpg?quality=51&w=1200)

- [Event website](https://rhythmsandthesummerbreeze2026.eventbrite.com/)
- [646–389–2332](tel:6463892332)

**An Evening of Caribbean Sounds, Food, Spirits & Good Vibes at Cityview Rooftop!**

An all-inclusive night of live musical talent from steel pan and violin to saxophone, we will also enjoy an intimate reggae performance by **Jemere Morgan, Grammy Award-winning artist and lead singer from Morgan Heritage**. After the live performances, we will turn up the vibes with selections from DJ Introvert.
