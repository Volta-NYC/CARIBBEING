**Key Responsibilities**

- Program coordination and relationship building with important customers.
- Work directly with customers to facilitate communication and maintain long-term strategic relationships.
- Work closely within multi-functional teams to identify new business opportunities and generate quotes to customers.
- Act as a mentor to support the company and provide direction and guidance on an as-needed basis.

**Basic Qualifications**

- Ph.D. in chemistry, pharmaceuticals, biology, or related fields.
- Strong organization skills: ability to handle multiple tasks and prioritize accordingly.
- Effective project management skills.
- Excellent interpersonal and communication skills.
- Skillful in writing, editing, and reviewing business documents.
