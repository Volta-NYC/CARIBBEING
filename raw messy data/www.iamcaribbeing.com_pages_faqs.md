# FAQs

### **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

**Q: When will my order ship?**

**A:** If your product is in stock, your order will ship within **3–5 business days** from your order date.

* * *

**Q: Do you accept returns?**

**A:** We do not accept returns unless the product was **damaged or defective** upon receipt.

* * *

**Q: Can I exchange my order?**

**A:** Exchanges are considered **on a case-by-case basis**. Reach out and we’ll do our best to accommodate you.

* * *

**Q: How can I get in touch with someone about my order?**

**A:** Email us at shop@caribbeing.com.

For a more immediate response, contact hello@caribbeing.com.

* * *

**Q: When will this item be back in stock?**

**A:** We produce in **small batches**, so restocks may take a little time. Once an item is restocked, we typically ship it within **2–3 business days**.

Thank you for your **patience, understanding, and for supporting a BLACK-OWNED BUSINESS.**

* * *

**Q: I can’t wait — I need my product NOW!**

**A:** If your order hasn’t shipped yet, we’re happy to issue a **full refund**. Just email shop@caribbeing.com.

Prefer a swap? Ask us about a **substitution**, like the same item in a different color or style.

As we say in the Caribbean, **“Patience is virtue and virtue is grace.”**
