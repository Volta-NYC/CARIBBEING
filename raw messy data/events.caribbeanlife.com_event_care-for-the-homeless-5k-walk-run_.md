# Care For the Homeless 5K Walk/Run

November 7, 9 am – 10:30 am.

![Screenshot 2026-04-17 at 9.22.08 AM](https://events.amny.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/05/Screenshot-2026-04-17-at-9.22.08%E2%80%AFAM-e1777860033354.png?quality=51&w=1195)

- [Event website](https://careforthehomeless.org/events/cfh-5k-runwalk-2026/)
- [212–366–4599](tel:2123664599)

Every Step Helps End Episodes of Homelessness  – Join us for a fun morning of community at our 5K Walk/Run in Riverside Park on Saturday, November 7th! Whether you’re walking, running or cheering from the sidelines, this is a chance to come together with friends, family, and community members to help end episodes of homelessness.

Kids Fun Run, refreshments, and awards, This is a perfect event for company and family/friend teams, individuals, and a great way to spend a morning showing your support for those experiencing homelessness. Last year, Care For the Homeless provided 821,250 meals, provided more than 850 individuals with a safe place to sleep, and provided healthcare to over 12,000 individuals with over 43,000 health care visits. As one of the nation’s first providers of healthcare for people experiencing homelessness, our integrated model continues to set us apart and is needed now more than ever.

Be part of a meaningful morning where every step counts. Every dollar raised helps ensure someone has access to care, a safe place to sleep, and the support needed to move from crisis to stability.
