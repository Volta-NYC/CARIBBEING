### **🍃** **Jenesis Caribbean Green Seasoning**

_20 oz_

Unlock the essence of _🌴_ Caribbean cooking with every all-natural sprinkle. Introducing ourCaribbean green seasoning blend, now crafted in convenient dry form for the first time. This all-purpose blend is behind the vibrant, honey flavors that make Caribbean cuisine unforgettable. Now you can infuse your favorite dishes with the authentic, bold taste of tradition and bring the heart 🧡 of the Caribbean to your kitchen. _Great on all savory dishes._ 🥘

**Ingredients** 🌿

Garlic onion, thyme, bell peppers, cilantro, parsley, chives, ginger, celery, vegetable oil, and habanero flakes.

### **🍃** Koko Kreole - Creole Blend

Our Creole seasoning is a versatile spice mix. It works on meats 🍗, seafood 🦞, chicken, side dishes & Vegetables 🥗. It also tastes great in gumbos, stews , soups & Sauces 🍲 . _A pinch of this blend is the love_ 💛 _your kitchen has been missing_.

**Ingredients 🌿**

Hand blended from paprika, granulated onion, granulated garlic, oregano, basil, thyme , black pepper, white pepper, cayenne pepper and celery seed.

### **🍃** Vincy Spice - Curry Powder

_40 oz_

Dedicated to the land of my birth, St. Vincent & the Grenadines, where my love 💛 for cooking and Caribbean flavors begun. _This_ _rich blend_ _can be added to_ _meats_ 🍗, _chicken_, _seafood_ 🦞, _vegetables_ 🥗, _stews_, _curries_ _& as an all purpose seasoning.🌶️_

**Ingredients 🌿**

Coriander, turmeric, mustard fenugreek seed, cumin, cinnamon, red pepper flakes, ginger, black pepper, and cardamom.

### **🍃** Kookie Love - Steak & Thangs Seasonings

_50 oz_

Dedicated to my love 'Kirk Adams'. This blend adds a pronounced flavor to steak 🥩 , chicken, pork, and vegetables 🥗. _It's especially useful for seasoning burgers_ 🍔 _and beef dishes giving them an extra flavorful kick!_ This seasoning offers a truly bold and robust taste that is unlike any other. _Use as a dry rub or turn into a Marinade._

**Ingredients 🌿**

Sea salt, pepper, garlic, onion, canola oil, crushed red pepper, coriander, dill weed, rosemary, thyme.

### 🍃Resi Blendz Citrus Infused Salt

_5.50 oz_

Delightful citrus 🍊 flavor with just a hint of smokiness from the Smoked Sea Salt🧂. _This delectable seasoning can be used on fish chicken, pork , shrimp, stir fries, roasted vegetables and even on the rim of a margarita glass_ 🍸 _._

**Ingredients🌿**

Hand blended from sea salt, orange. black pepper, lemon, lime juice powder (organic tapioca Maltodextrin, Organic Lime Juice Concentrate, and Lime Oil), and ginger.
