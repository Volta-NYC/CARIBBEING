## Meet Kimberly

**Kimberly Waldropt is the founder of TERRA-TORY, a plant-based body care brand rooted in ritual, resilience, and craftsmanship.**

With a background in fashion and experience inside high-pressure corporate environments, Kimberly knows what burnout feels like in the body. Long hours and chronic stress disrupted her immune system, triggering dyshydrotic eczema and severe skin irritation.

What began as survival became devotion.

Drawing from soapmaking techniques she learned as a child from her Caribbean mother, Kimberly returned to her hands. She began crafting cold-process soaps and nutrient-dense body butters using plant oils and botanicals that supported her compromised, dry skin.

What started in her kitchen as a personal remedy evolved into TERRA-TORY, a brand designed for women who do it all and need products that truly replenish. Each formula is intentionally crafted to combat post-shower dryness, support the skin barrier, and transform daily hygiene into grounding ritual.

Kimberly builds TERRA-TORY at the intersection of discipline, softness, and heritage. Her work honors ancestral knowledge while serving the modern working woman who deserves care that restores rather than strips.

## Why We Don't Use Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is a great moisturizer in its pure form. But when converted into soap, it becomes a highly cleansing agent that can strip the skin’s natural barrier, leaving it **dry, itchy, and prone to irritation**. While not “bad,” we prioritize **skin-friendly alternatives** that care for sensitive skin daily.

## Ingredients You Can Eat

We use clean, earth-grown ingredients, guided by the seasons and what’s accessible and sustainable. Our formulas are built with skin-loving superfoods like fresh aloe vera, cactus, carrots, ginger, sea moss, and avocados to nourish skin from the inside out.

## Raw Lather / Never SLS

We never use SLS or synthetic lather boosters. These ingredients can strip the skin over time, worsening dryness and irritation like eczema. Instead, we rely on plant-powered ingredients to create a rich, creamy lather that moisturizes and supports a healthy skin barrier.

## Essential Oils

Our scents come exclusively from high-quality, plant-based essential oils. We never use synthetic fragrances. Unscented means truly unscented. No essential oils included.
