## Discover a new way to search on YouTube

We’re experimenting with a new way to search on YouTube that feels more like a conversation. Users can ask complex questions in their own words, receive comprehensive results that include video and text, then ask follow ups to dive deeper.

How to use it:
Enter a prompt into the search bar, such as “plan a 3-day road trip between San Francisco and Santa Barbara” and select ‘Ask YouTube’ to get your results. Try follow-up questions or select suggested prompts to explore in more detail.

Available on desktop for Premium users 18+ in the US searching in English.

Quality and accuracy may vary. You can share feedback to help us improve by selecting either a thumbs up or thumbs down at the bottom of the search response, and optionally provide a rationale about your experience.
