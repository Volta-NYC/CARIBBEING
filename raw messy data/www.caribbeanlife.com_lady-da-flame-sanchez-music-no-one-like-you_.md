# Lady Da Flame, Sanchez unite for ‘No One Like You’

![](https://www.caribbeanlife.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/12/carib_square_2013_300.png)By [Nelson A. King](https://www.caribbeanlife.com/author/nelson-a-king/ "Posts by Nelson A. King")Posted on [May 5, 2026](https://www.caribbeanlife.com/lady-da-flame-sanchez-music-no-one-like-you/)

International recording artist Lady Da Flame has delivered a standout moment with “No One Like You (Remake)”, featuring reggae legend Sanchez.

Caribbean entertainment publicist Flair Lindsey of Acclaim PR told Caribbean Life on Monday, May 3, that the release is “a seamless, cross-generational collaboration that brings together two powerhouse voices in reggae.”

She said the single leads Lady Da Flame’s newly-released EP, “The Journey,” that’s now available on digital platforms worldwide.

“Built on rich melodies and heartfelt delivery, ‘No One Like You (Remake)’ blends classic reggae soul with contemporary strength,” Lindsey said. “Lady Da Flame’s commanding vocals glide effortlessly alongside Sanchez’s signature tone, creating a timeless sound that resonates across eras.

She said Lady Da Flame’s “The Journey” EP is “a spiritually ground project” with three songs.

“Sanchez is extremely talented and professional; so, it felt like a breeze,” Lady Da Flame said.

With a shared vision of creating uplifting music, Lindsey said the record was crafted to “bridge age groups and inspire listeners, reinforcing her mission to deliver songs that feed the spirit and stand the test of time.

“Already gaining strong momentum, the single is charting, garnering radio play and racking up endorsements from reggae greats,” she said.

Lindsey said the release also serves as “the perfect lead-in to an anticipated Mother’s Day performance” (Jammins Events) at New York’s UBS Arena alongside Beres Hammond, Stephanie Mills, and Romain Virgo, where Lady Da Flame is set to deliver “a powerful and memorable set honoring mothers and legacy.

“The single anchors ‘The Journey’ EP, produced by Rawborn Productions, a spiritually-grounded project described as inspiring, spiritual, artistic, insightful, profound, and deliberate,” Lindsey said.

Featuring “All Praises To The Most High,” “More Dan Prayer,” and “No One Like You (Remake),” she said the EP reflects Lady Da Flame’s personal growth and spiritual walk, “offering listeners a body of work meant to uplift, energize, and renew.”
