To maintain healthier skin, exfoliating is very important.

Dry brushing:

- Helps to remove dead, dull, rough, flaky skin cells.
- Supports your body’s natural revitalization processes for soft, smooth skin.
- Done weekly, can reduce the appearance of uneven skin tone.
- Known improve circulation and lymphatic drainage (reduce swelling, ridding of excess or unwanted fluids in the body)
- Plant-Based sisal fibers
- Bamboo hand and bristle base
- Hemp Rope to hang
- 100% organic cotton carry pouch

Dry brushing can be done daily, before or during your shower. Gently brush skin in an upward motion toward the heart, starting at the feet and hands and brushing upwards. But we like to go in random little circles all over the body. (No rules here, just be sure to enjoy yourself!)

_**Pro Tip**: After brushing, shower with any **[TERRA-TORY soap cube](https://terra-tory.com/collections/soaps)** of your choice, then use an all-natural moisturizer like our **[Multi-Purpose Butter](https://terra-tory.com/collections/body-butters)** to replenish your skin. Hang dry on our [**TT Silicone Shower Hook**](https://terra-tory.com/products/tt-silicone-no-trace-wall-hook-single) to keep dry and bacteria-free._
