**Jiggle Studio** isn’t your typical gym.

We’re a body-positive fitness studio that rejects diet culture and celebrates all bodies. Our tagline is: **Stay Fat Get Fit.**

We don’t do weigh-ins, transformation photos, or “bikini body” BS. We

create a space where movement is about celebrating what your body can

do—not changing how it looks.

Our community is people who’ve been

intimidated by traditional gym culture. Folks seeking mental health

benefits, strength, and fun—without the pressure to shrink themselves.

If you’re tired of the fitness industry’s toxic messaging and want to

teach kickboxing that’s actually empowering (not punishing), keep

reading.

**What you’ll teach:**

- Group kickboxing classes (all levels welcome)
- Basic punches, kicks, and combinations
- Proper form and technique
- Modifications for different abilities and bodies
- Focus on the mental health benefits of hitting things

**Responsibilities**

- Lead kickboxing classes that combine technique, cardio, strength training, and fun to maximize participant engagement
- Demonstrate proper form and techniques for punches, kicks, stances, and combinations while ensuring safety at all times
- Adapt workouts for different skill levels, including beginners and advanced students
- Monitor participant progress and provide personalized feedback to encourage improvement
- Maintain a clean and organized training space, ensuring all equipment is safe and ready for use
- Promote a positive attitude towards fitness and martial arts while motivating participants to achieve their goals

**Required:**

- Certified Group Fitness Instructor (ACE, NASM, AFAA, or similar)
- Experience teaching kickboxing or martial arts-based fitness
- Genuine passion for inclusive, body-positive fitness
- Excellent communication and motivational skills
- Ability to demonstrate and modify movements for all levels
- Understanding that kickboxing is about empowerment, not aggression

**Bonus Points:**

- Experience with beginner or gym-intimidated populations
- Knowledge of HAES (Health at Every Size) principles
- Background in martial arts or boxing
- CPR/AED certified
- Trauma-informed teaching approach
- You’re just a good human who makes people feel powerful

Work Location: In person
