## An Ode To Resilience

Six years of staying.

Staying when it felt uncertain, when it felt slow, when inspiration ran thin. Built without shortcuts, without outside funding. Just repetition, instinct and belief. As a founder and a mother, life has shifted in cycles--demanding more, asking for patience, stretching me in ways I didn't expect. But the rituals remained. The daily return to self, to craft, to care. This collection is a reflection of that rhythm--of that healing, pivoting, learning, and continuing anyway. A full circle moment. Not because it was easy, but because I stayed.
