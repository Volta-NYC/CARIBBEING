## What's included with YouTube Premium?

YouTube Premium offers an ad-free experience across all of YouTube, YouTube Kids, and YouTube Music. Premium memberships include YouTube Music Premium, a standalone app where you can listen to over 100 million songs ad-free. [Learn more](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/15968883#background_play).

YouTube Premium also includes access to some other features:
• Picture-in-picture: Watch music content with picture-in-picture when using other apps
• Jump ahead: Get to the content that you care about faster
• Continue watching: Resume watching where you left off even when you switch between devices
• High-quality video: Get enhanced bitrate 1080p for extra crisp and clear videos with lots of detail and motion
• High-quality audio: Enjoy a richer listening experience at 256kbps when watching eligible music videos
• Smart downloads: Get recommended videos automatically added to your downloads for offline viewing
• Queuing: Set up videos to watch next without interrupting your current watch session

## What's included with YouTube Premium Lite?

Premium Lite benefits (background play, downloads, ad-free) available on most non-music content, excluding Shorts. Ads may appear when you search or browse. [Learn more](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/14286363).

## What’s the difference between Premium and Premium Lite?

Premium Lite's features apply to most non-music content on YouTube. This means you'll be able to watch your favorite podcasts and creator videos ad-free, offline, and in the background.

Ad-free streaming, background play, and downloads aren't available for music-focused content like official music videos, covers, and dance videos. [Learn more](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/14286363).

Premium unlocks all of YouTube and YouTube Music ad-free, offline, and in the background. Plus, enjoy exclusive Premium features across all content without interruptions.

## How do I download videos and music?

You can download and watch videos and music on your mobile device using the YouTube app, the YouTube Music app, or the YouTube Kids app. You can also download videos and watch on your computer using Chrome, Edge & Opera browsers.

You can watch or listen to content offline for up to 30 days without being connected to the internet.

[Learn more about how to watch videos offline](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/11977233)

## How do I play videos and music in the background?

Background play is on by default for YouTube, YouTube Music, and YouTube Kids. This means if you're watching a video on YouTube and you leave the YouTube app, the video will keep playing in the background until you pause it. You can turn off background play anytime in your settings.

[Learn more about background play](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/6308116?visit_id=637904039924932250-4198970444&p=red_benefits&rd=1#zippy=%2Cbackground-play)

## How do I add people to my plan?

You can share your YouTube Premium membership with up to 5 additional household members with a family plan. Once you get a family plan, you can add members if you’re the family manager of your plan. Family members sharing a family plan must live in the same household as the family manager and have a Google account. Each family member gets their own private, personalized account.

[Learn more about our family plan](https://support.google.com/youtube/answer/7507744)

## How can I cancel my membership?

Cancel your membership by visiting the [Purchases and memberships](https://www.youtube.com/paid_memberships) page. You can rejoin Premium or Premium Lite at any time.
